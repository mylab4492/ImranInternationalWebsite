About Us

Imran International

IMRAN INTERNATIONAL is a reputable recruitment firm that operates with dynamism and professionalism in GULF recruitment field. As an ISO 9001:2015 certified company with registered number (Reg no B-0575/mum/part/1000+/5/9048/2013) approved by the Ministry of External Affairs in India, we have gained recognition as a preferred recruitment partner and trusted consultant for overseas clients.

Since its establishment in the year 2000, Imran International has been led by the visionary Chairman, Mr. Imran Ahmed Khan. Under his guidance, we have emerged as a nationally renowned recruitment brand. Our dedicated team, comprising highly qualified professionals, manages the entire manpower sourcing process seamlessly, allowing our clients to concentrate on their core competencies.

Over the years, Imran International has achieved remarkable milestones in a relatively short span of time. We pride ourselves on delivering quality and reliable manpower solutions, catering to the needs of various skill levels, including skilled, highly skilled, semi-skilled, and unskilled individuals, across Gulf Countries.

Our commitment to excellence and our ability to provide top-notch recruitment services have positioned us as a trusted partner in the industry.
