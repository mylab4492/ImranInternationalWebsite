About Us

Imran International

Imran International epitomizes professionalism and excellence as a distinguished recruitment firm specializing in Gulf recruitment. With ISO 9001:2015 certification and goverment approval from the Ministry of External Affairs in INDIA with the Registration Number
(B-0575/mum/part/1000+/5/9048/2013), we are esteemed as a trusted recruitment partner for overseas clients.

Since our inception in 2000, under the visionary leadership of Chairman Mr. Imran Ahmed Khan, we have emerged as a nationally renowned recruitment brand. Our dedicated team of highly qualified professionals ensures seamless management of the entire manpower sourcing process, enabling our clients to focus on their core competencies.

Imran International has achieved remarkable milestones over the years, priding ourselves on delivering quality and reliable manpower solutions tailored to diverse skill levels across Gulf countries.

Our commitment to excellence and unparalleled recruitment services have solidified our position as a trusted industry partner. Our comprehensive offerings include Recruitment, Wakala services, Visa Stamping, Emigration Clearance, and Flight Tickets, providing our clients with unparalleled support delivered with utmost professionalism and reliability.
